Auto Encoder



The Auto Encoder will output the sentence again to the original sentence and star rating. Therefore, when the learning is completed, the information about the sentence and the star is combined. We can use this to create a generation model that changes the Style of a given sentence according to a star rating. The higher the score, the more positive the sentence. The lower the score, the more negative the sentence.
